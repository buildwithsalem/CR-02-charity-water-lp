Our Story

charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We believe that access to clean water is a basic human right, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality for millions around the world.

Since our founding in 2006, we have funded over 100,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing clean water to over 13 million people. Our approach is simple: we partner with local organizations to implement sustainable water solutions that meet the unique needs of each community.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
